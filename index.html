Brands

The brand 'NAMA' has been registered in over 50 countries with 15 tire patents. With high reputation in tire industry, NAMA has been supplying tires to customers all over the world in the past years. NAMA is supported by the international professional R&D team, among them the main technical personnel are from major brands of United States. With full product line and size range, NAMA has successfully occupied a place in tire markets from PCR, TBR to OTR, STR, IND Agriculture series. With years of development, in order to better expand the US market, besides original produced in China lines, NAMA has been growing with non-Chinese production lines in Vietnam, Thailand, Pakistan, and Europe. Produced in Industry 4.0 factory with world-class production equipments, NAMA tires are with premium quality along with unique tread pattern designs.

CHAMPTYRE, as the official representative export company of Jilin Jixing Tire Co., Ltd. is one of the most professional tires suppliers in China, our main brands GRIPOWER, TEKPRO and LUCKYLAND PCR tires are engineered by European technology with quality warranty.

We have been serving customers from America, Europe, Asia, Middle East and Africa for years and earned great reputation from them. We can produce & design new tire pattern/size in OEM project and after-sales market as well.

CHAMPTYRE pays much attention to establish cooperative relations with the friends of business. It hopes through this trade tie, to make the further connection between domestic market and oversea market.
